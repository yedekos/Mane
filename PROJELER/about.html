Mane Ethical Hairdressing is committed to creating a salon that exists in harmony with our environment.

We pride ourselves on our low-tox environment. Our salon is family friendly so we want to ensure it is a safe and pleasurable place for our clients and their children.

We are committed to working with products that align with our values of sustainable and ethical processes. Where we can, we will always support Australian and local businesses.

We promise to take the time to work with you to achieve the hair style you want without compromising your hair integrity and strive to provide you quality service always.

Mane Ethical has been accredited by Sustainable Salons Australia. We have committed ourselves and put in place the processes to ensure every possible measure is taken to create a minimal waste salon.

MEET BRONWYN CASSELLS. Founder and Senior Stylist

Bronwyn has over 25 years experience in hairdressing and has worked for some of Australia's most reputable hair salons. It was at these salons she was exposed to the ethical and sustainable opportunities available within the salon industry.

Motherhood was formative in her true adoption of a low-tox lifestyle. She was convinced that salon products should be safe for all ages and stages of life. After years of research, testing and trialing she found the products that matched her values and better yet, gave her clients the results they wanted.

Family is incredibly important to Bronwyn and therefore having a family friendly salon was a non-negotiable. It was important to create a place where parents can enjoy the salon experience with their children.

The space Bronwyn has created at Mane Ethical Hairdressing has been done purposely to encourage a community of similar values.
